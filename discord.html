Our Discord Server

Information about our discord server and how to join

How do I join?

Please ask an admin+ privately or in our clan chat for the instant invite to our server, to keep a safe environment for everyone, the ability to invite is reserved for admins

What can I expect from the discord server?

Well, first of all, the discord is monitored to a high standard and is a safe welcoming environment for all to chat and meet new friends

We host a variety of daily events ranging from PvM, skilling or social activities. A list of events can be found HERE on our events calendar

We also have a monthly clan newsletter to showcase our members achievements and a channel for people to tell a little about themselves and introduce themselves if they feel comfortable to do so

Don't be shy, come on in, the water is fine!
