Welcome to The LGBT Corner.

A place for LGBT+ Runescapers from across the globe to get together, play games, chat, have a laugh and make friends!

Join Us. We Have Ice Cream! Check out The LGBT Corner on - RuneScape.com or RuneClan.

Community Focused Clan.
"The Colourful Corner of Runescape"

There are plenty of accepting clans out there for LGBT+ players but no others that are specifically for LGBT+ players and allies, this clan was created to change that, and we have successfully been around for over 3 years.

Nothing is mandatory in this clan except being kind to one another, everyone is welcome regardless of level. We treat our guests the same as our members.
